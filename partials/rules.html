Rules

1

Timescaling, lagging, cheating records

Any kind of timescaling, lagging or cheating on any record will result in a permanent ban immediately without warning.
2

Exploiting

If a player finds an exploit in a map that has been never found for example; glitched boosts, getting on top of a stage, lamp bhop etc. then it should be reported to an admin immediately. Breaking a record with a new exploit will result in a possible permanent ban without warning.
3

Preboosting, prehopping, doubleboosting

Any kind of preboost, prehop or doubleboost on a map which has a velocity cap will result in a possible permanent ban without warning.
4

Modifying your client

Any kind of client modification to gain an unfair advantage for example: Speedhacking, cvar bypassing and so on will result immediately in a permanent ban without warning.
5

Crouchboosting

Any kind of crouchspamming to hit a push trigger multiple times will result in a possible permanent ban without warning.
6

Multiple accounts

You are not allowed to finish in any top10, or else all accounts will result in a ban up to a permanent ban except the main account without warning.
7

Spamming

Any kind of voice or say chat spam will result with a warning, a kick or even a possible ban.
8

Negative attitude

Repeatedly negative attitude and/or toxic behaviour will result in a kick or possible permanent ban without warning.
